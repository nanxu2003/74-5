Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Business websites typically take 2-3 weeks from brief to launch. Custom development projects vary by complexity but generally range from 4-8 weeks.

What if I'm not satisfied with the design?

We work iteratively with you at every stage (wireframes, UI concepts). We include three rounds of comprehensive revisions in our standard package to ensure your complete satisfaction.

Do you provide source code and design files?

Absolutely. Upon final payment, we transfer all design source files (Figma/Sketch) along with complete frontend and backend source code repositories.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes, all projects include 12 months of complimentary technical support. We also offer flexible retainer packages for ongoing updates and feature development.
